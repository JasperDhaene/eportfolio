About

Hi! My name is Jasper D'haene and I'm a student of life. Most of the time, I'm located somewhere in Ghent, Belgium. Luckily, this is very convenient, because I'm currently enrolled at Hogeschool Ghent, studying Applied Computer Science. Before studying at Hogeschool Ghent I went undercover for three years at the University of Ghent. Unfortunately, I had to reveal my true identity when I found out I wasn't cut out to be an academically trained Computer Scientist. I've been fascinated by computers since I was in elementary school, so I'm pretty thrilled I can now tell them to do stuff. In my free time I'm known to enjoy a good story, on a preferably very large visual medium, all the while savouring a good whiskey. Or reading up on some (eastern) philosophy. Or cruising on my longboard (but not when it's raining).
